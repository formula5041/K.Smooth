Our track in Taiwan

Members Party - Taipei

We invite all members of our company to participate in parent-child parties held regularly every single month. And provide free meals, funny games and other services. On the day of the event, the birthday children of the month will also be invited to the stage to celebrate their birthdays and present the company’s products in a lucky draw.

Environmental - Taichung

K.Smooth's products are made of pure natural cypress in Taichung and Heping area, and the wood in that area is planted by our company for many years. Over the years, not only has an area of 200 hectares of forests been created, but also an endless cycle of ecological environments has been created.

Soprts Day - Tainan

Every year, K.Smooth organizes an employee sports meeting, inviting employees and relatives within the company to participate. The content includes baseball, basketball, volleyball, fun games, etc.... Exercise makes employees healthier and can increase the cohesion of the team.

Education Pool - Taitung

K.Smooth has long cared for children in remote areas, and actually established schools in areas with poor educational resources, and donated part of the surplus to disadvantaged families in the area. Most importantly, we have also developed a special API special education system for the physically and mentally handicapped to provide local students free of charge.
